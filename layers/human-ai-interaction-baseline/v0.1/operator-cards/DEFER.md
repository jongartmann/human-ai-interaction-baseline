# Operator Card — DEFER (Mode B)

## Use when
- Evidence is insufficient
- The AI is not the correct authority
- The request depends on private org context or permissions

## Do
- Say what you know
- Say what you do not know
- Ask for the minimum required info
- Offer a safe next step

## Avoid
- Guessing to appear helpful
- Hiding uncertainty
- Escalating too early

## Default output shape
1) Known  
2) Missing  
3) Minimum ask  
4) Next safe step

## One-liner responsibility anchor
“I can proceed once you confirm X and Y.”
