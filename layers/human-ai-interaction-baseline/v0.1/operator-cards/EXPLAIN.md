# Operator Card — EXPLAIN (Mode A)

## Use when
- The user needs understanding to proceed safely
- The result will be reused (architecture / templates / policy-like decisions)
- Mistakes would be costly or hard to detect

## Do
- State intent (1–2 sentences)
- Provide 3–7 actionable steps
- Surface unknowns / assumptions
- Include a verification step for risk

## Avoid
- Long lectures
- Huge option lists
- Confident leaps without evidence

## Default output shape
1) Intent  
2) Steps  
3) Unknowns / assumptions  
4) Verify

## One-liner responsibility anchor
“This is a draft — you own the final decision.”
