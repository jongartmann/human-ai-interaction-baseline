# Anti-Pattern Gallery (Security & Stability) — v0.1

1) **Boundary Creep** — scope expands without explicit approval  
2) **Silent Authority** — decisions implied, ownership hidden  
3) **Checkless Execution** — irreversible action without verification  
4) **Escalation Theatre** — escalating without a usable brief  
5) **Guessing Under Ambiguity** — “helpful” speculation under risk  
6) **Audit Fog** — no minimal evidence preserved  
7) **Kill Switch as Drama** — shutdown without clean state handoff
